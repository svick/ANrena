Arena is a drafting format where a series of randomized "packs" of four cards are presented to you. Each card you pick is added to your deck. This generator has a certain set of constraints that will lead to a legal deck with a minimum number of essentials.

Start by picking your side. Try to make the best deck you can. And then go over to jinteki.net to find a partner. Enjoy!

If you'd like to learn more about drafting in Netrunner, click here: Cube Drafting 101!
